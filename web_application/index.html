What is Explainable AI?

When AI models make decisions (like identifying objects in images), we often want to understand why they made that decision. Explainable AI (XAI) provides tools to visualize what the model is "looking at".

üîç Heatmaps

A heatmap is a visual representation that highlights which parts of an image the AI model considers most important for its decision. Brighter/warmer colors indicate higher importance.

The Jet Colormap

Low importance Medium High importance

Blue = Low attention (cold) ‚Üí Green/Yellow = Medium attention ‚Üí Red = High attention (hot)

Example: Understanding a Heatmap

Heatmap for "Cat"

In this example, when asked about "Cat", the heatmap shows warm colors (red/orange) concentrated on the cat. This means the model is correctly focusing on the cat region to identify it.
